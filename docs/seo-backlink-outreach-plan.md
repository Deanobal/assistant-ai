## Rules

Do not buy bulk backlink packages.
Do not use private blog networks.
Do not mass-submit to low-quality directories.
Do not use exact-match anchors every time.
Do not publish fake awards, fake partner logos, or fake customer claims.

Use a natural anchor mix:

- AssistantAI
- AssistantAI resources
- AI receptionist resources
- AI receptionist Australia guide
- missed call automation guide
- AI receptionist buyer checklist
- Australian service business automation
- https://www.assistantai.com.au/Resources

## Target categories

### Tier 1: Highest value

- Australian small business publications
- Australian trade business publications
- cleaning industry blogs and associations
- plumbing/electrical business blogs
- property management and real estate operations blogs
- Australian startup and SaaS directories
- podcast guest pages for SMB growth, AI, automation, and trades
- partner pages from web design, CRM, marketing, and automation agencies

### Tier 2: Useful foundation links

- chamber of commerce profiles
- local business directories
- AI tool directories
- SaaS directories
- founder profile pages
- supplier resource pages
- startup showcase pages
- local business networking group listings

### Tier 3: Only if quality is acceptable

- general directories with real moderation
- niche resource pages
- community business profiles
- event speaker pages

Avoid any site that is full of casino, adult, crypto spam, spun content, scraped posts, or obvious paid link farms.

## Weekly outreach target

Minimum weekly target:

- 25 prospects researched
- 15 outreach emails sent
- 5 follow-ups sent
- 2 new listings/submissions completed
- 1 partner/resource link secured

Monthly target:

- 8 to 15 relevant backlinks
- 2 to 4 partner links
- 2 guest/resource placements
- 4 to 8 directory/profile links

## Prospect qualification checklist

Only outreach if the site passes most of these:

- Relevant to Australia, AI, small business, trades, service businesses, CRM, marketing, SaaS, or operations
- Real traffic or real audience signal
- Human-written content
- Clear contact method
- No obvious link spam
- No unrelated gambling/adult/crypto content
- Page could naturally reference AssistantAI

## Backlink quality notes

One relevant link from an Australian service-business publication is more useful than 100 junk links.

Backlinks should support trust and discoverability. They should not be treated as a shortcut around weak content or poor conversion.
